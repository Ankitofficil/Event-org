FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does a table booking actually work?

One pass = one reserved table at one event. Pick the event, pick a tier (Standard / Premium / VIP), and your table is held for you and your guests for the whole night. Just show the QR at the door.

How many people fit on a table?

Standard seats 4, Premium seats 6, VIP seats 8. Need a bigger table or want to combine two? WhatsApp us and we'll arrange it.

Why do prices change between events?

Each event has its own headliner, venue, production budget, and demand. Bigger nights (galas, headline DJs) carry higher table rates. The starting prices on this page are the floor — check the event page for the exact rate.

How do I get my pass after booking?

After payment confirmation, you'll receive a QR code via WhatsApp and email. Show this at the venue entrance — one scan and the whole table walks in. No printouts needed.

Can I transfer my booking to someone else?

Yes — the table is what's reserved, not the person. You can hand the QR to whoever's leading your crew that night, as long as the lead guest carries valid ID. WhatsApp us at least 24 hours before to update the lead name.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before the event. 50% refund for 24–48 hours. No refunds within 24 hours of the event. If we cancel the event, full refund within 48 hours.

Is there an age limit?

Most events are 18+. Family-friendly events (like Acoustic Dusk) are open to all ages. Check each event listing — valid government ID required at entry for the lead guest.

Can I book a private event or buy out a venue?

Absolutely. We offer custom packages for birthdays, corporate outings, college fests, and engagement parties. WhatsApp us with your guest count and budget — we'll send a tailored quote.

What payment methods are accepted?

UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, debit/credit cards via Razorpay. Cash at select partner outlets.

Is there a dress code?

Themed events (Black Tie Gala, etc.) have a stated dress code on the event page. For general events, smart-casual is recommended. No slippers or torn clothing at premium venues.
