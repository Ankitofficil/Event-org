### Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I get my pass after booking?**
After payment confirmation, you'll receive a QR code via WhatsApp and email. Show this at the venue entrance for instant check-in. No printouts needed.

**Can I transfer my pass to someone else?**
Single-event Starter passes can be transferred up to 24 hours before the event by contacting us on WhatsApp. Monthly Pulse and VIP passes are tied to your identity and are non-transferable.

**What's the refund policy?**
Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before the event. 50% refund for 24–48 hours. No refunds within 24 hours of the event. Monthly pass cancellations take effect the next billing cycle.

**Is there an age limit?**
Most events are 18+. Some family-friendly events (like Acoustic Dusk) are open to all ages. Check the specific event listing for age requirements. Valid government ID is required at entry.

**Can I book a private event?**
Absolutely. We offer custom event packages for birthdays, corporate outings, college fests, engagement parties, and more. Head to the Contact page or WhatsApp us with your requirements and budget.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, debit/credit cards, and cash at select partner outlets. All online payments are processed securely via Razorpay.

**Is there a dress code?**
It depends on the event. Themed events (Black Tie Gala, etc.) have specific dress codes mentioned on the event page. For general events, smart-casual is recommended. No slippers or torn clothing at premium venues.

**What happens if an event gets cancelled?**
Full refund processed within 48 hours to your original payment method. Monthly pass holders get a bonus event added to their next cycle.

7. **Contact / Book Now** — Enquiry form, contact info, WhatsApp integration, Google Maps embed

### Mobile Optimization Checklist
- Responsive grid: single-column on mobile, multi-column on tablet/desktop
- Hamburger menu for navigation on screens < 680px
- Touch-friendly buttons: minimum 44px tap targets
- Sticky bottom CTA bar on mobile ("Book Now" always visible)
- Optimized images: WebP format, lazy loading, srcset for different screen sizes
- Font scaling: use `clamp()` for responsive typography
- Swipeable event cards on mobile (carousel)
- Bottom sheet for filters on mobile events page

### Recommended Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js or React + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express or Django
- **Database:** PostgreSQL or MongoDB
- **Payments:** Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking — India standard)
- **Auth:** Firebase Auth or OTP-based login via WhatsApp
- **Hosting:** Vercel or AWS (Mumbai region for low latency)
- **CDN:** Cloudflare for images and static assets
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 + Meta Pixel
- **CRM:** WhatsApp Business API for automated confirmations
- **Email:** Resend or SendGrid for transactional emails
