Our Story

From House Parties to City Stages

What started as a group of friends throwing house parties quickly grew into Jamshedpur's most trusted event collective. We noticed the city had venues with potential but no one connecting them to the right audience with the right experience. PULSEPASS bridges that gap.

What We Do

End-to-End, Done Right

We handle everything — venue sourcing and booking, sound and lighting production, artist and DJ coordination, security and crowd management, pass distribution and digital check-in, food and beverage partnerships, and post-event content for our community.
